A NEW TRENDY YET CASUAL BURGER BAR OPENS ITS DOORS IN AMSTERDAM'S

Right in the heart of Amsterdam’s most famous and trendy disctruct The happening scene of young urbans in ‘De Pijp’ as the perfect setting for this new concept. A Stylish burger bar serving top-quality burgers, prepared on the spot using only the freshest ingredients without losing perspective of good value for money. The relaxed atmosphere of THE BUTCHER and the professional service level make for the ideal trendy yet relaxed meal out.

The chefs choose to work only with the best quality meat: Aberdeen Angus beef thus committing to creating the ultimate burger. From the choice of meat, to the selection of herbs, the freshest vegetables, the magic signature sauces, right down to the homemade bun, we stand for quality, quality and more quality.

QUALITY AND INNOVATION

The striking facade of our place matches its surroundings perfectly. As if it were a traditional butcher, the selection of meat is displayed in the window. What you see, is what you get: top quality burgers, prepared on-the-spot with a wide variety of choices. Whether you prefer a traditional burger or something more contemporary and adventurous, this is the place to go.

Attention to detail at all levels, from presentation of menus to food wrappings, napkins and more gives it a distinctive tone of voice and standout appearance in Amsterdam.
